History:

Nike, Inc. is an American multinational corporation that is engaged in the design, development, manufacturing, and worldwide marketing and sales of footwear, apparel, equipment, accessories, and services. The company was founded on January 25, 1964, by Phil Knight and Bill Bowerman.

Mission:

To bring innovation and innovative products to consumers while remaining true to the brand's heritage of creativity, excellence, innovation, and commitment to sustainability.

Products:

  • Footwear: Sports shoes, running shoes, casual shoes, etc.
  • Apparel: T-shirts, shorts, hoodies, jackets, etc.
  • Equipment: Running equipment, football equipment, basketball equipment, etc.
  • Accessories: Watches, bags, socks, etc.

Sustainability:

Nike has implemented various sustainability initiatives in recent years, including the Nike Social Innovation Fund, the Sustainable Innovation Lab, and the Reuse-a-Bag program. The company has also pledged to use recycled or sustainable materials in all its products by 2025.
